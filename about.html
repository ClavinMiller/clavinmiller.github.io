Purpose / 网站用途

一份持续生长的工程档案

中文

这个网站不是简历的加长版，也不是商业作品集。它用于完整保存一个工程项目从动机、环境、实现到失败与迭代的过程，让过去的判断能够被检索，让新的工作建立在真实积累之上。

网站展示机器人、嵌入式系统和智能控制方向的项目记录；ROS2、ESP32、视觉追踪与边缘 AI 的技术笔记；项目影像、生活片段和个人兴趣。它同时承担公开主页、实验日志和长期个人档案三种角色。

English

This is not an extended résumé or a commercial portfolio. It is a maintained record of how engineering work develops—from motivation and environment setup to implementation, failure, and iteration.

The archive presents projects in robotics, embedded systems, and AI control; field notes on ROS2, ESP32, visual tracking, and edge AI; and a visual record of projects, everyday life, and personal interests. It serves as a public homepage, a lab notebook, and a long-term personal archive.
